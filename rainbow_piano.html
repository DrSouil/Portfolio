Rainbow Piano

Rainbow_piano from hengshi kang .

本作品使用MR平台对MIDI键盘进行追踪并把它的位置属性传输给Unity3D。Unity3D把MIDI键盘输入的MIDI信号转化为声音并且根据它的位置和按键情况生成对应的笔刷和它的颜色。就像用钢琴画画。

This work use MR platform to track the position of MIDI keyboard and pass the position data on to Unity3D software. Unity3D transfer the MIDI signal to sound and render the drawing and its color according to keyboard's position and key. It's just like drawing with piano keyboard.
