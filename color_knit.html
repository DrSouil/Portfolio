Color knit

本作品使用Processing对图形进行处理和生成动画。以传统的“编织”为灵感，提取使用者上传图像的像素颜色，以“编织”的动画生成对应的图像。

This work use Processing to process the images and produce animation. Inspired by the traditional "knitting", I made it to extract the pixel of the uploaded image and generate the corresponding animation.
