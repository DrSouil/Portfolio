Mixed Reality

Unity3D, Oculus Rift, MR platform

April-June 2017

About

This work is the application of MR (Mixed Reality) platform in design, innovation and user research for smart vehicle cockpit. Making the best of MR platform, I innovatively designed and built a series of physical tools. They can be used by user to change the materials and forms of the cabin components, set and modify the elements like screen and light in the virtual scene. It combined the entity interaction and the virtual scene very well. After studying the hardware and software system of MR platform, I made the data transmission of entity design tools and co-design, multiple users can design in this environment.
