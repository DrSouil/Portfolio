Proprioception

VR and haptic experience

Unity3D, Arduino, HTC Vive

November 2018, Collaborated with Yiming Yang

About

This project is a VR cat whisker experience that engages human with cats’ proprioceptive ability.
We came up with the concept together and my partner did the metal wire structure “the whiskers”. Then at each side of it I attached two vibration motors which will vibrate according to the VR scene. The motors are controlled by Arduino which linked to Unity3D.
