About

Hengshi Kang is a digital artist/designer/developer whose work explore a wide spectrum of media include motion graphic, sound/graphic interactive installation, AR/VR and AI. By using emerging technologies, he plays a role as magician to challenge viewer’s intuitive and anti-intuitive and bring them the experience that can wake their inner child. He received his B.Eng. from Tongji University in 2018 and is currently a 2020 MA candidate at Royal College of Art in Information Experience Design.
